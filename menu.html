The Menu

A fresh flavour

Dive into our menu

Welcome to Serena, where culinary artistry meets timeless elegance. Our menu is a carefully curated celebration of the seasons, using only the freshest local ingredients. Every dish is crafted with precision, blending classic and contemporary techniques to create a dining experience that is both refined and inviting. We believe that great food begins with quality, care, and a passion for every detail on the plate.

Begin your journey with our beautifully prepared appetizers, followed by main courses that showcase bold flavors and global influences. From fresh seafood and perfectly aged meats to vibrant plant-based creations, each dish is designed to satisfy and inspire. Complete your experience with one of our handcrafted desserts , thoughtfully chosen to complement the essence of every meal we serve.
